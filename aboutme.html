Presentation

Dr. phil. Mag. jur. Alexandra JUSTER

Born in Austria, PhD in German Modern Literature from the University of Salamanca (Spain), doctor in law of the University of Ferrara (Italy), Master in Education of the University of Navarra (Spain), Master in School Leadership from the University José Camilo Cela Madrid (Spain). Lawyer. Postdoc researcher in German and Compared Literature. Fluent in German mother tongue, French, Ebglish, Italian and Spanish.

Fields of investigation: Modern literature and interdisciplinary fields at the crossing of literature, law, philosophy and society (anthropocene, exile, migration, memory, gender and violence).
