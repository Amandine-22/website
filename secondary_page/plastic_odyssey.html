Slide 2 - Overview

Overview

Plastic Odyssey – an innovative project by Simon Bernard and Alexandre Dechelotte to combat the global plastic waste crisis through local recycling

Core Concept

  • Mobile recycling facility on board a ship
  • Three-year worldwide expedition
  • Transformation of plastic waste into new products
  • Training of local entrepreneurs

Strategy: Show – Train – Transform – Multiply
